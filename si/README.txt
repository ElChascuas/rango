Our page has a display of alternatives, where you can opt for different products, news / articles of the different heroes and villains and of course access the characters in the activities.
 
In the previously exposed urls you can see the options that the user has.
Each of them has two options. The first where you can see the list of the different categories. And in a second, whoever has access can create more in it.
http://127.0.0.1:8000/products/list/   Here are all the products already exposed and uploaded to the web.
http://127.0.0.1:8000/products/create_product/  In this url the option is given to those who have the power, to create new products and upload them.
http://127.0.0.1:8000/blog/articles/  In it you see the list of articles and news about the fascinating characters and new activities.
http://127.0.0.1:8000/blog/create_article/  Whoever has the authorization can create new content.
http://127.0.0.1:8000/characters/characters/  The characters, their characteristics and abilities are revealed.
http://127.0.0.1:8000/characters/create_character/  Whoever has the power, creates new characters and makes them known in this new world.
